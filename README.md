## About The Project

This repository will showcase an authentic business problem in which I will analyse multiple different high-quality datasets, cleaning data, creating figures to support new found insights from my analysis. Additionally, I will build a professional data analysis workflow to develop a solution. This final project has also supported my upskilling in creating testing suites for my scripts linking CircleCI so when changes are pushed, I can see whether or not my tests that I have written for each piece of code has passed or not.

In this case, my chosen business problem aims to cover marketing within social media. The rise of social media platforms, short-form content and constant entertainment creates the ideal environment for certain industries to advertise their business or products. However, not all business can benefit from this due to demographic of users on certain platforms as well as the correct focus on target audience. Therefore, conducting effective analysis on the use of social media platforms today, who uses them and what industries could benefit could shape the way business move forward with their marketing. 

Finally, I aim to create a solution using Artificial Intelligence where industries can receive targetted and concise information on the best method of advertisement on social media including optimal platforms and examples for hooks (in the case of short form videos) for example.
